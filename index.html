FRIENDS AVENUE CAFE

A fresh blend of Australian, English and European favorites, all competitively priced. A carefully selected free style breakfast menu and a lunch menu that consists of freshly made gourmet sandwiches, crunchy colorful salads and delicate main courses.

MELT BURGER COMPANY

Young, Free and Homemade. Burger Melt Company is a new, mouthwatering concept from the kitchen of Friends Avenue Cafe. It is intended to change the way how you eat burgers. Our burgers are insane, different and all hand crafted.

AÇAI AND THE TRIBE

Açaí and the Tribe is another project from Friends Avenue Cafe, to bring you the best of Açaí and wholesome bowls. We use the highest grade açaí in the market. We are passionate about healthy, sustainable and beautiful eating.
